Forest Quest Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how MADONE Studio collects, uses, and protects information when you use Forest Quest.

Effective date: May 7, 2026 Last updated: May 7, 2026 Package: com.madone.forestquest

1. Introduction

MADONE Studio is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes what data may be collected while using Forest Quest and how that information is used in line with applicable platform and legal requirements.

2. Information We Collect

Forest Quest may process limited technical and gameplay-related data to operate the app and improve player experience.

  • Device and app diagnostics, such as app version, crash logs, and performance signals.
  • Advertising identifiers (where available) for ad delivery and fraud prevention.
  • Gameplay progress data stored locally or in cloud-linked services, depending on enabled features.

3. How We Use Information

Data is used to provide core gameplay functions, maintain app stability, analyze quality metrics, deliver relevant in-app ads where configured, and prevent abuse or technical misuse.

4. Third-Party Services and Sharing

Forest Quest may use third-party SDKs for analytics, crash reporting, and ad mediation. These providers process data under their own privacy terms. MADONE Studio does not sell personal data.

5. Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as required to support app operations, legal obligations, and dispute handling, then deleted or anonymized based on technical and legal constraints.

6. Security

MADONE Studio applies reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect data against unauthorized access, misuse, and accidental loss. No method of transmission or storage is fully secure.

7. Children's Privacy

If the app is directed to children or used by children, additional safeguards and compliance controls are applied according to Google Play Families Policy and relevant child privacy regulations.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your region, you may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your data. You may also limit ad personalization through your device settings where supported.

9. Changes to This Policy

MADONE Studio may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any update will be posted with a revised last updated date in this document.
