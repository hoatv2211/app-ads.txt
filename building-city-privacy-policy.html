MADONE Mobile Game Development Privacy and Data Transparency

Building City — Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how MADONE handles information when you use Building City.

Effective date: May 7, 2026 Last updated: May 7, 2026 Package: com.madone.buildingcity Platform: Android (Google Play)

1. Overview

MADONE (“we”, “us”, or “our”) built Building City as a free mobile city-building game. We are committed to protecting your privacy. Building City does not collect, store, or transmit personally identifiable information (PII) to MADONE servers. All game progress is stored locally on your device.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Building City does not directly collect the following for MADONE servers:

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Precise or approximate location data
  • Device phone number or IMEI
  • Contacts or account lists from your device
  • Photos, media, or files from your device

3. Third-Party Services

Building City integrates third-party SDKs that may collect technical or usage data automatically under their own privacy policies.

3.1 Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob (Publisher ID: ca-app-pub-2218023806663419) to display in-game advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (Google GAID) for ad personalization and fraud prevention
  • IP address for approximate location determination and ad delivery
  • Device information such as model, OS version, and screen size
  • Ad interaction data such as impressions, clicks, and engagement duration

To opt out of personalized ads on Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.2 Unity Analytics

Building City is built with Unity Engine, which may process analytics and diagnostics data including:

  • Session information such as play sessions and duration
  • Device information such as model, OS version, and hardware profile
  • Gameplay events (for example: level progress, building placement)
  • Crash and stability diagnostics

Unity Privacy Policy: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

3.3 Google Play Billing (In-App Purchases)

If you make in-app purchases, transactions are processed by Google Play Billing. Payment details are handled entirely by Google — MADONE never receives or stores your payment information.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Local Data Storage

All game progress, settings, and in-game currency are stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences or local files.

  • This data is not uploaded to any external server.
  • This data may be lost if you uninstall the app or clear app data.
  • This data is not shared with third parties by MADONE.

5. Android Permissions

Building City requests only the following permissions:

PermissionPurpose
INTERNET Load advertisements and perform connectivity checks
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE Detect network availability before loading ads
ACCESS_WIFI_STATE Detect Wi-Fi connection status

The app does not request sensitive permissions such as camera, microphone, contacts, location, or phone state.

6. Children’s Privacy

Building City does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 through MADONE servers. The game may display advertisements through Google AdMob.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us at madonestudiogame@gmail.com so we can take appropriate action.

7. Data Retention

Because MADONE does not maintain server-side personal data for Building City, no direct data retention period applies to MADONE records. Data processed by third-party providers (AdMob, Unity) is subject to those providers’ own retention and deletion policies.

8. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, delete, opt out of, or obtain a copy of your personal data. To exercise rights regarding third-party data:

  • Google: https://myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy
  • Unity: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

For direct MADONE support: madonestudiogame@gmail.com

9. Third-Party Links

Advertisements may link to third-party websites or applications. MADONE is not responsible for the privacy practices of those services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected by revising the Effective Date shown at the top of this document. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
